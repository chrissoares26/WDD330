Start small then go big

  • Be There
  • Be Useful – Do what you are supposed to do
  • Be Quick – No one will be there forever. Interaction smooth. Light app – Less steps

Be aware of reachability

Speed matters.

Use SVG. Optimize and Minify CSS and JS files

Placement matters. Be careful where I am placing the controls on mobile

On a form, less is better.

Use number keypad when only required numbers

Use tel tag
